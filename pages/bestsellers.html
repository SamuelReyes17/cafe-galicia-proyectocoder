Prospect

If you're seeking a balance of adventurousness and comfort, our originav bl blend is for you. Prospect brings together carefully sourced coffees from our partners across East Africa and Latin America, yielding a well-rounded brew laden with fresh, fragrant florals and crisp caramel sweetness.

Origin

Region
